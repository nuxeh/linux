PWM (1)
=======

Set SW5 to 2-3 to monitor the PWM1 signal on the backlight pwm connector or
monitor pin 2 on SW5. Ideally 200ns/div or 500ns/div

To export device:

$ cd /sys/bus/platform/devices/e6e31000.pwm/pwm/pwmchip0/
$ echo 0 > export

To start:
$ cd pwm0
$ echo 1000 > period
$ echo 500 > duty_cycle
$ echo 1 > enable

change width to 25:75
$ echo 250 > duty_cycle

The driver does not support the polarity inversion by
$ echo 0 > enable
$ echo inversed > polarity
$ echo 1 > enable


PWM (2)
=======

To get PWM2, monitor pin2 on SW6

to export pwm:
$ cd /sys/bus/platform/devices/e6e32000.pwm/pwm/pwmchip1
$ echo 0 > export

to start
$ cd pwm0
$ echo 1000 > period
$ echo 500 > duty_cycle
$ echo 1 > enable

this will produce a 1000ns repeated square wave of 50:50 duty.


Notes
-----

The sysfs-api is at: https://www.kernel.org/doc/Documentation/pwm.txt
